Hi, I'm Esat Ozen!

When I took the photo above, the year was 2016, the season was spring, Slovenia was rainy, and Bled was lovely. And I was a plucky reporter in Turkey who was married for 3 months. Right after, a non-stop adventure has been started for me. I've visited more than 7 countries, lived on two continents, experienced different work areas, learned cultures, traditions, languages... And in the end, I found myself where it all started; numerical sciences.

Nowadays, I am putting into use all the different and original practices that I have experienced with my mathematical skills for the best development practice. I love creating functional, responsive websites and applications. Creating new algorithms, diving deep into the complex problems, and finding the hidden answers are my favorite hobbies. For the time being, I am residing in Winchester, MA, and enjoying life with my lovely daughter and wife. And still traveling all around the world!
